Introduction

Who is Peanut?

Peanut is a Jack Rusell/ Pinscher cross. Because of this she is very small and is often asked; "are you a puppy?", even though she is nearly eight years old.

Where is Peanut?

Peanut lives in Clonmel with her owner's mother, as unfortunately she was not allowed to move with her owner to college since she now lives in a small apartment in Dublin with a "no dogs allowed" rule.

How is Peanut?

Peanut is often said since her owner is away during the week, but Peanut becomes very happy when her owner returns for the weekend to hang out with her.
